Get To Know Me

Hi there, my name is Seoyun!

I was born in Seoul, South Korea and moved to the US when I was six. My family found a pleasant suburban house in Mill Creek, Washington and we have been living there ever since. Fortunately, all the schools I attended were near my house. Mill Creek Elementary School, Heatherwood Middle School, and Henry M. Jackson High School, the schools I have/are attending, are all a short drive away from where I live.

Currently, I am a senior at Jackson High School. It’s been a good three years, but I’m especially excited for senior year. I’ve slowly become involved in lots of activities to keep me busy this upcoming year. I participate in my school’s swim team and tennis team. I am also in NHS and TSA.

I like to do a lot of things in my free time, but I especially like to go hiking and camping; if it’s anything related to nature and the outdoors, I’m down to do it. My favorite hiking spots are Wallace Falls and Heather Lake. Two of my good friends and I have a hiking group, and we call ourselves the Sherpals. I also love taking pictures, growing plants, going thrift shopping, and volunteering. I volunteer at my local animal shelter every weekend and it’s my favorite thing ever!
